The Society for Creative Anachronism

Posted by Robert McAnulty on September 5, 2013

The SCA is an international organization dedicated to researching and re-creating the arts and skills of pre-seventeenth century Europe. The SCA "Known World" consists of 19 kingdoms, with over 30,000 members in countries around the world. Members, dressed in clothing of the Middle Ages and Renaissance attend events which feature tournaments, royal courts, feasts, dancing, educational classes and workshops, and much more. (Copied off the Kingdom of the West website)

Hats

The hats can be plain rolled brim style, or decorated with flowers of various kinds, or buttons of various kinds. There are berets, and open topped hats in plain or complicated styles, as well as something I think of only as headbands, along with a slightly different style of "ear warmers" that could be considered a type of hats too.

Scarves

There are several sizes and styles of scarves to choose from, and if the colour does not appeal, then let us know and we'll make one to suit. Adult scarves are slightly longer and wider than those for children, and the length can be varied to suit personal taste. Natural fibres are available on request.

Bookmarks

These bookmarks are made by hand, of 100% organic cotton fibres, and are fully machine washable. They are also acid free, so that use will not damage the pages of your books, and are available in a variety of styles.

Blankets

These blankets are available in every size from baby blankets to Queen size, and are completely machine washable. Custom orders are accepted and appreciated, but take a bit longer to make. Up to 3 month are required to create the largest sizes, but baby blankets will be available for next day shipping.

Dresses

There are several different sizes, lengths, styles and colours of dresses presently available, and custom orders are greatly appreciated. winter weight and summer weight materials in stock. Natural fibres are available on request. Send measurements and I will do my best to make them form fitting.

Flowers

We have multiple different kinds of flowers available. They can be added to any other work, be attached to a hair-clip or a hat, or even just as a stand-alone decoration. Orchids, roses and daffodils are currently available. With the variety of colours and fibres available, any combination could work. In any decorating situation there will be something that can be improved by the addition of a flower or two.

Ladies, if your 'man' is too manly for the brighter colours, try Green Camouflage or Grey, or even Silver.

Gloves

Also available are many different styles of gloves and mittens, in both adult and children's sizes. Men's and women's sizes and bright and subdued colours are in stock, including a limited stock of black fingerless gloves. Perfect for keeping your hands warm while riding or working outside, but still having the mobility of your fingers.

Bags

Based on the original design for the baby hats, these medium sized bags can hold almost anything; from your sewing kit to more than a gallon of dice, from pens and pencils to a collection of hand held games perfect for keeping the kids entertained on those long road trips. Perfect for keeping lots of small things in the same location too.
